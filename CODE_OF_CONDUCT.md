# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Duckle to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and reviews.
- Assume good intent; critique ideas and code, not people.
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it kindly.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces - issues, pull requests, discussions,
and any other official channel - and when representing the project in
public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer at souravroy7864@gmail.com.
Reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers may
remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, and other interactions
that violate this Code of Conduct, and may temporarily or permanently ban
any contributor for behavior they deem inappropriate.
